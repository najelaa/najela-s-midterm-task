Get To Know About Us

KOPIKIMI GOLD is a coffee shop was established in 2020 with a vision of giving consumers the best coffee drinking experience possible. We are committed to offering high-quality coffee that has been meticulously and wholeheartedly processed.

We also provide a variety of non-coffee drinks, snacks and dessert. Kopikimi Gold is more than just a coffee shop; it's a nice spot to rest, work, and interact. We provide a warm and welcoming atmosphere with a modern and minimalist interior design.
